2. Description of Service

Seeker Verify is a free companion app for Solana Seeker device owners. It provides:

  • On-chain verification of Seeker Genesis Token (SGT) ownership
  • Display of SKR token balance, staking, and reward information
  • SOL balance and staked SOL display
  • .skr domain resolution and display
  • On-device Season 2 airdrop tier prediction based on on-chain activity
  • Community fleet position and statistics

The App is a read-only tool. It does not create blockchain transactions, manage private keys, or hold custody of any assets.
